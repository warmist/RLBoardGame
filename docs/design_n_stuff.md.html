# Design doc A random assortment of game ideas and mechanics thrown together. # Mechanics ## Wound system Instead of health have wound cards that get added to the deck or hand or discard. Some interesting features: * Worst damage gets added to hand, a bit less bad to deck and least problematic to discard * The cards could have effects themselves - Auto destroy - some minor wound - Do something bad (e.g. add more wounds) - bleeding? - Need AP to remove - Do not discard at the end * Lose if you have e.g. 3 wounds in your hand * Healings could remove wounds from hand, then draw, then discard ## Items Initial idea was to have all cards added because item gives them (e.g. sword add 3 strike cards, 1 parry, ...) that has nice features and makes it easy to have the deck building by manging items. Games that already do that: [guild of dungeneering][GoD] and [card hunter][ch]. Other random ideas: * Item damage that makes it loose cards * Item crafting/modification so you further customize your deck ## Skills This is a twist (or modification) on the items idea. You could get cards from items but they only provide POTENTIAL. You need skill to get those cards. This would work like this: Sword (3 fighting, 1 finesse) gives you only 1 card until you raise your skills. Some unanswered questions: * How do you raise skills? * How to learn new skills? Another idea: using item lets you add X cards to your deck. Using can be: killing enemies or times used or times discarded or times destroyed etc... # Misc * Persistence: do not generate new world each time. Let reexplore the same one. - Raise-dead/resurrect old chars/collect their loot - Somehow remix the world a bit? Collapse some walls, maybe dig some more? - Maybe portal idea links to this * The map is constantly growing by adding stuff from center outwards. Center is fixed but everything else get slowly (each death/run) further from center. - Makes retrieving things harder - Makes map more interesting as explored areas mix with new ones * Cards that can be played from the deck/discard: Good idea or not? * Class/race gives the passives that change how it's played (i.e. [slay the spire artifacts][sts]) * Item/creature world? - Tweak decks by entering item worlds. - Resurrect creatures by battling their death inside them * Room cards? Room shards? some way to add/expand the map * Magic vs Swords vs Ranger vs ??. Can be differentiated by: - more single card vs more multi card/combos - more item gives cards vs using item unlocks cards - card mechanics: cards that are just "use-discard-draw" vs "use to add cards to deck-destroy" vs "on discard do X" vs "only when X happens do sth" vs etc... - wounds: magic might be harder with wounds, fighting easier? ## Todo * Sounds * Card movement animations * Lua-ify everything * Card layout problems - Not enough text fits - Some ascii art? - Links to mechanics * Gui problems - Ugly bad actions position * Card ideas and possible mechanics * Deck building ideas and rethink ## References * [Guild of Dungeoneering][GoD] * [Card hunter][ch] * [Slay the spire][sts] * [Book of demons][bod] * [Deep sky derelics][dsd] * Cultist simulator(?) * Dominion board game(?)
